YouTube Growth System — 5 Steps
+

The exact system used to hit 300+ subscribers and 1,000+ watch hours in 2.5 months. Not theory — this is what actually worked.

1
Content Audit
Check retention %, cut what isn't working. Don't build on a broken foundation.
2
Keyword Architecture
3-tier: broad discovery, mid-tail pillars, long-tail search-intent titles. Every video needs a keyword home.
3
Hook Engineering
First 30s determines watch time. Create curiosity gaps — eliminate "why should I keep watching?"
4
Thumbnail + Title A/B
Consistent system, test one variable. Track CTR weekly, not monthly.
5
Community Loop
Reply to every comment in first 48 hrs. Build habits, not just views.
